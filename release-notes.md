## 🎬 LocalhostMiniOTG v1.0.0

**Stream videos, photos, and music from your PC to any device on your LAN.**

### What's included

- 🎥 **Video streaming** - MP4 direct play + MKV/AVI/WMV auto-transcoded via HLS (GPU-accelerated)
- 📸 **Photo viewer** - JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP + RAW support (CR2, NEF, ARW, DNG) with thumbnail grid
- 🎧 **Music player** - Dedicated /Music page with playlist, shuffle, repeat, and **iPhone lock screen controls**
- 📁 **File browser** - Browse drives/folders from the web UI, breadcrumb navigation
- ⭐ **Favorite folders** - Bookmark music folders for one-tap access
- 🔧 **Zero config** - Single .exe, auto-opens browser, auto-configures firewall, shows LAN IP

### Download

1. Download `LocalhostMiniOTG-v1.0.0-win-x64.zip`
2. Extract anywhere
3. (Optional) Place `ffmpeg.exe` and `ffprobe.exe` next to the exe for MKV/RAW support
4. Run `LocalhostMiniOTG.exe`
5. Open the Network URL on your phone

### Requirements

- Windows 10/11 (x64)
- No .NET runtime needed (self-contained)
- FFmpeg optional (for MKV/AVI streaming and RAW photos)
